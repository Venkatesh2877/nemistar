Why use compostable bags ?

Better for the Planet

Compostable bags break down into natural materials like water, carbon dioxide, and organic matter — leaving no toxic residue behind. Unlike plastic, they return safely to the Earth.

Reduce Plastic Pollution

Every year, millions of tons of plastic end up in landfills and oceans. Compostable bags offer a sustainable alternative, helping reduce this harmful waste.

Made from Plants, Not Petroleum

Most compostable bags are made from renewable resources like cornstarch or vegetable oils — not fossil fuels — making them a cleaner, greener choice.

Safer for Wildlife

Plastic can take centuries to decompose and often harms animals. Compostable materials break down quickly and naturally, reducing risk to marine and land animals.

Aligns with Eco-Conscious Living

Using compostable bags is a simple yet powerful way to live more sustainably and inspire others to do the same.
