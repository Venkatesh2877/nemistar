About Us

Creating a Lasting Alliance for a Greener Future

At Nemistar, we are committed to creating a cleaner, greener planet—one bag at a time. As a leading manufacturer of 100% compostable bags, we specialize in producing eco-friendly alternatives to conventional plastic that are durable, compostable, and fully sustainable.

Founded on the belief that innovation can drive environmental change, we use cutting-edge technology and materials derived from renewable resources such as cornstarch, sugarcane, PLA (polylactic acid), and PBAT to ensure our bags break down naturally without leaving harmful residues. From retail and grocery to industrial and custom packaging solutions, our products are designed to meet diverse needs while aligning with global sustainability goals.

Together, let’s build a future where convenience doesn’t cost the Earth.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: reduce plastic waste and support a circular economy. Through responsible manufacturing practices, continuous R&D, and close partnerships with eco-conscious businesses, we aim to make biodegradable packaging the new standard.

Our Values

We are dedicated to protecting the planet by reducing plastic pollution and promoting eco-friendly alternatives. Every bag we produce is designed with nature in mind.

We conduct our business with honesty, clear communication, and ethical responsibility—building trust with our partners, customers, and the communities we serve.

We prioritize the needs of our clients by offering tailored solutions, responsive support, and a shared commitment to sustainable progress.

Our Story

Built with heart, Backed by vision

In a world drowning in plastic, we chose a different path—a cleaner, greener one.

In 2023, our journey began with a simple yet powerful belief: everyday choices can create a lasting change. We saw the damage single-use plastics were causing to our planet, and we knew there had to be a better alternative. That’s when we committed ourselves to create bags that do more than carry—they care.

Today, we proudly make 100% compostable bags that break down naturally, leaving no toxic trace behind. From grocery runs to garbage bins, our bags are designed to serve their purpose—and then return to the earth, just as nature intended.

This isn’t just about bags. It’s about responsibility. It's about redefining convenience without compromising the future.

Because when we take care of the planet, it takes care of us.
